# Using the Shared Kiosk Tablet to Clock In and Out

This article explains how to use the shared kiosk tablet at your restaurant to record when you start and end your shift. It covers entering your PIN, confirming your identity with a selfie, recording your tips, and what happens when the tablet is offline.

## Before you begin

- Your manager must have set up a PIN for you before your first kiosk punch. If you do not have a PIN, ask your manager to create one for you.
- The kiosk must already be in **Kiosk Mode** — your manager launches this from the Time Punches page. If you see the normal app instead of the kiosk clock-in screen, let your manager know.

## Clock in at the start of your shift

1. Walk up to the shared tablet. You will see the **Kiosk Mode** screen with an **Enter PIN** card and a live clock.
2. Tap the number buttons on the keypad to type your PIN. The PIN field shows dots instead of the actual digits to keep your PIN private. Your PIN is between 4 and 6 digits.
3. Tap **Clock In**. A **Verify Your Identity** dialog opens.
4. The camera turns on automatically and shows a live preview. Look into the camera.
   - Tap **Confirm punch** to take a selfie and record your clock-in. The photo is attached to your punch to help prevent time disputes.
   - Tap **Skip photo** if you prefer not to use the camera. Your punch is still recorded.
5. A green confirmation banner appears with your name, "Clocked in", and the time. The PIN field clears automatically so the next person can use the kiosk.

## Clock out at the end of your shift

1. On the **Kiosk Mode** screen, type your PIN using the number buttons.
2. Tap **Clock Out**. The **Verify Your Identity** dialog opens.
3. Tap **Confirm punch** (to include a selfie) or **Skip photo**.
4. After your punch is recorded, the **Enter Your Tips** dialog opens automatically.

### Enter your tips after clocking out

The **Enter Your Tips** dialog appears right after a successful clock-out.

1. In the **Cash Tips** field, type the dollar amount of cash tips you received today.
2. In the **Credit Card Tips** field, type the dollar amount of credit card tips you received today.
3. When the combined total is greater than zero, a **Total Tips** amount is shown.
4. Tap **Submit Tips** to save your entries. Your manager will review and include the amounts in the tip pool.
5. If you did not receive tips or want to skip, tap **Skip**. No tip entry is recorded.

## Correct a typo in your PIN

- Tap the **X** button (backspace) on the keypad to erase the last digit you typed.
- Tap **Clear** to erase all digits at once and start over.

## What happens after too many wrong PIN attempts

After 5 incorrect PIN attempts, the keypad locks for 60 seconds. A "Locked" badge with a countdown appears on the screen. Wait for the timer to reach zero, then try again. If you have forgotten your PIN, ask your manager to reset it.

## Set your own PIN after a manager reset

If your manager has reset your PIN (for example, if you forgot it), you will be prompted to choose a new one immediately after your first successful punch.

1. A **Set Your Personal PIN** dialog opens after the punch is confirmed. It cannot be dismissed — you must set a new PIN before the kiosk returns to the main screen.
2. Type a new PIN (between the minimum number of digits shown and 6 digits) in the **New PIN** field.
3. Type the same PIN again in the **Confirm PIN** field. A "PINs match" message appears when they are identical.
4. Avoid simple sequences such as 1234, 9876, or 1111 — the system will warn you if your choice is too predictable.
5. Tap **Save New PIN**. Your new PIN is active immediately for all future punches.

## Clock in or out when the tablet is offline

The kiosk saves punches locally if the tablet loses internet connection. You will see the message "Saved offline — will sync when online" after your punch is recorded. The indicator at the bottom of the screen also shows how many punches are waiting to sync.

When the connection is restored, the queued punches are sent to the server automatically — no action is required from you. Your hours will appear in the system as soon as the sync completes.

## Tips

- Always wait for the green confirmation banner before walking away. It confirms your punch was recorded (or queued offline).
- Keep your PIN private. Do not share it with other team members — each person has their own PIN.
- If the camera shows an error, tap **Skip photo** to record your punch without a selfie.
- The clock on the kiosk screen is always live, so you can confirm the time of your punch before tapping **Clock In** or **Clock Out**.
- The **Submit Tips** button is disabled when both tip fields are zero or empty. Tap **Skip** if you received no tips.

## Troubleshooting

**The keypad is greyed out and I cannot tap any buttons.**
The keypad is locked after 5 failed PIN attempts. Wait for the "Locked" countdown to reach zero (up to 60 seconds), then try again with the correct PIN.

**I tapped Clock In but got a message "You are already clocked in."**
The system shows this when you have an open shift that was not closed. Ask your manager to review your time punches.

**I tapped Clock Out but got a message "No open shift to clock out."**
This usually means your last clock-in was not recorded, or you already clocked out. Ask your manager to check your punch history.

**The camera shows an error or a black screen.**
Tap **Skip photo** to record your punch without the photo. Let your manager know about the camera issue so it can be resolved for future shifts.

**The PIN I was given is not working.**
Check that you are entering the correct digits. If the problem continues, ask your manager to reset your PIN.

**The kiosk screen shows "Launch kiosk from Time Punches" instead of the clock-in screen.**
Kiosk Mode has not been started on this device. Ask a manager to launch it from the Time Punches page.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need an internet connection to clock in?**
No. If the tablet goes offline, your punch is saved locally and syncs automatically when the connection is restored. You will see a confirmation message on screen.

**Why does the camera open when I tap Clock In or Clock Out?**
The selfie helps verify that you — and not a coworker — are recording the punch. This protects your earnings and keeps payroll accurate. You can always tap **Skip photo** if you prefer not to use the camera.

**What if I forget to enter my tips?**
The tip dialog appears immediately after clocking out. If you tap Skip or close it, the tips are not recorded for that punch. You may need to ask your manager to enter them manually.

**Can I use my own phone instead of the shared tablet?**
The Kiosk Mode screen is designed for a shared, manager-configured tablet. For clocking in on your own device, see [Clock In, Start a Break, and Clock Out](/help/employee-time-clock).

**What happens if someone else uses my PIN?**
Each PIN is tied to a specific location. If you think someone else has used your PIN, let your manager know immediately. Your manager can reset your PIN from the Time Punches page.
