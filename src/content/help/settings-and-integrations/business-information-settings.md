# Add Your Business and Tax Information

The **Business** tab in Restaurant Settings is where you store your legal business name, entity type, EIN, and registered address. This information is used when printing checks, generating invoices, and setting up payment features — so keeping it accurate matters.

## Before you begin

You must be signed in as an **owner** or **manager** to see or edit the Business tab. Staff and other roles do not have access to this section.

## Open the Business tab

1. In the left navigation, go to **Settings**.
2. At the top of the Settings page, click the **Business** tab.

The tab contains two sections: **Entity Details** and **Business Address**.

## Fill in your Entity Details

The **Entity Details** section captures your legal identity and tax information.

1. In the **Legal Name** field, type your business's full legal name exactly as it appears on your tax documents (for example, "Sunrise Grill LLC").
2. Open the **Entity Type** dropdown and choose the option that matches your business structure:
   - Sole Proprietor
   - LLC
   - Corporation
   - S Corporation
   - Partnership
   - Non-Profit
3. In the **Business Email** field, enter the email address you use for billing and official business correspondence (for example, `billing@restaurant.com`).
4. In the **EIN** field, type your nine-digit Employer Identification Number. As you type, the app automatically formats it as **XX-XXXXXXX** — you only need to enter the digits.

## Enter your Business Address

The **Business Address** section is used on printed checks and invoices.

1. In **Address Line 1**, enter your street address (for example, `123 Main St`).
2. In **Address Line 2**, enter a suite, unit, or floor number if applicable. This field is optional.
3. In **City**, enter your city name.
4. In **State**, enter the two-letter state abbreviation (for example, `CA`). The field automatically converts your entry to uppercase.
5. In **ZIP**, enter your ZIP or postal code (up to 10 characters).
6. In **Country**, enter the two-letter country code. This field defaults to `US` and automatically converts your entry to uppercase.

## Save or discard your changes

Once you have filled in the fields:

- Click **Save Changes** to save your updates. A confirmation message will appear when the save is successful.
- Click **Reset** to discard any edits you have made since your last save and restore the previously saved values. The Reset button is only active when there are unsaved changes.

## Tips

- The EIN field accepts digits only — no dashes needed. The XX-XXXXXXX formatting is applied automatically.
- The **State** and **Country** fields accept only two characters and automatically capitalize what you type, so you cannot accidentally enter an invalid length.
- All fields are optional individually, but completing them fully ensures that checks, invoices, and payment setup screens pull in the correct details without manual corrections.
- If the **Business** tab is not visible in your Settings, you are signed in with a role (such as staff) that does not have access to edit business information. Ask your restaurant owner or manager to make changes.

## Troubleshooting

**The Business tab is not visible.**
Only owners and managers can see this tab. If you are an owner or manager and still cannot see it, sign out and sign back in, then check that the correct restaurant is selected at the top of the page.

**The Save Changes button is grayed out.**
The button is only active when you have made at least one change that differs from what was last saved. If you have not edited any field, or if you clicked Reset, the button will remain disabled.

**I see a "Permission denied" error when I try to save.**
Your account role may not match what the system expects. Contact the restaurant owner to confirm your role is set to owner or manager.

**The EIN field shows only numbers, not the formatted version.**
If you see raw digits without the dash, refresh the page and re-enter the EIN. The XX-XXXXXXX format is applied as you type after the second digit.

## Frequently asked questions

**Who can edit business information?**
Only users with the owner or manager role can view and edit the Business tab. Other roles do not see this tab in Settings.

**Where is this information used in the app?**
Your legal name, address, and EIN appear on printed checks, customer invoices, and during payment account setup. Keeping these fields accurate ensures your documents and payment profile reflect the correct details.

**Is the EIN stored securely?**
Yes. EIN and other business details are stored in your restaurant's secure account record and are only accessible to owners and managers of that restaurant.

**Can I leave some fields blank?**
Yes. No field in the Business tab is required before you can save. You can fill in fields gradually as you gather the information. However, leaving fields blank means that feature (such as check printing or invoicing) may show incomplete information.

**What if my entity type is not in the list?**
The six options — Sole Proprietor, LLC, Corporation, S Corporation, Partnership, and Non-Profit — cover the most common US business structures. If none fits your situation, leave the field blank and consult your accountant for guidance on how to classify your business.
