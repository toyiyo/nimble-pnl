# Sign In or Create Your EasyShiftHQ Account

This article walks you through creating a new EasyShiftHQ account and signing back in on future visits. It applies to owners, managers, chefs, and staff members.

## Before you begin

No special permissions are required to create your own account. If a manager or owner has already invited you to join their restaurant, follow the invitation link in your email instead — see [Accept a Team Invitation](/help/accept-team-invitation).

## Create a new account

1. Go to the EasyShiftHQ sign-in page (`/auth`).
2. Click the **Sign Up** tab near the top of the card.
3. Fill in the three fields:
   - **Email** — enter the email address you want to use.
   - **Full Name** — enter your first and last name.
   - **Password** — create a secure password.
4. Click **Sign Up**.
5. Check your inbox for a verification email and follow the link inside to confirm your address.

Once verified, you will be signed in automatically and taken to the app.

## Sign in with Google

You can use your Google account instead of a password — this works on both the **Sign In** and **Sign Up** tabs.

1. Go to the sign-in page (`/auth`).
2. Click the **Continue with Google** button at the top of either tab.
3. Follow the Google sign-in prompt in the window that opens.
4. After Google confirms your identity, you will be taken to the app.

No separate password is needed when you use Google.

## Sign back in with email and password

1. Go to the sign-in page (`/auth`).
2. Make sure the **Sign In** tab is selected.
3. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
4. Click **Sign In**.

If you have forgotten your password, click **Forgot password?** (shown to the right of the Password label) and follow the instructions to reset it. See also [Reset or Change Your Password](/help/reset-change-password).

## Dismiss the Welcome modal and start your free trial

The first time you sign in, a **Welcome to EasyShiftHQ** dialog appears. It confirms that you are starting with **Pro features FREE for 14 days** — no credit card is required to begin.

The modal shows the three available plans so you can see what each one includes after your trial ends. When you are ready to continue:

1. Click **Get Started** at the bottom of the dialog.

The dialog closes and you land on the main Dashboard. You can review or change your plan at any time in Settings — the modal itself notes "You can change your plan anytime in Settings."

## Sign out

Your email address is displayed in the footer at the bottom of the left sidebar.

To end your session:

1. Look at the bottom of the sidebar for your email address.
2. Click **Sign Out** directly below it.

You will be returned to the sign-in page. If the sidebar is collapsed to icons only, the Sign Out button appears as a small icon in the same footer position.

## Tips

- **Bookmark the sign-in page.** Go directly to `/auth` whenever you need to sign in.
- **Use Google if you already have a Google Workspace account** — it avoids managing a separate password.
- **Your trial starts immediately** after your first sign-in. You have 14 days of full Pro access before needing to choose a plan.
- **Only one email per account.** If you receive a team invitation, use the same email address you registered with so your access is linked correctly.

## Troubleshooting

**I clicked Sign Up but nothing happened.**
All three fields — Email, Full Name, and Password — are required. Make sure none are empty, then try again.

**I did not receive a verification email.**
Check your spam or junk folder. If it is not there after a few minutes, try signing up again with the same email to trigger a new message.

**The app says my email or password is incorrect.**
Double-check for typos in your email address. Passwords are case-sensitive. Use **Forgot password?** on the Sign In tab to reset your password if needed.

**I see a message about SSO.**
Some organizations require sign-in through a company identity provider. If you see that notice, contact your IT administrator or restaurant owner for the correct sign-in link.

**I missed the Welcome modal.**
The modal only appears once. You can still review plan details and upgrade at any time through Settings inside the app.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need a credit card to sign up?**
No. Your 14-day Pro trial starts the moment you create your account, with no payment information required upfront.

**Can I use both Google and email/password to sign in?**
If you created your account with Google, continue using Google to sign in. If you registered with an email and password, use those credentials. Mixing the two methods for the same account is not supported.

**How long is the free trial?**
Your trial gives you full Pro features for 14 days. After that, you can choose the plan that fits your restaurant — Starter, Growth, or Pro.

**Can I change my plan after the trial ends?**
Yes. The Welcome modal confirms "You can change your plan anytime in Settings." See [Choose or Change Your Subscription Plan](/help/subscription-plans) for step-by-step instructions.

**What if someone else invited me to their restaurant?**
If you received an invitation email, follow the link in that email rather than creating a brand-new account. See [Accept a Team Invitation](/help/accept-team-invitation) for details.
