# Choose or Change Your Subscription Plan

This article explains how to review your trial or subscription status, compare the three EasyShiftHQ pricing tiers, switch between monthly and annual billing, and start the checkout process to activate a paid plan. This applies to restaurant owners only.

## Before you begin

Only users with the **Owner** role can see the **Subscription** tab in Settings. Managers and other team members do not have access to billing. Make sure you are signed in with an owner account before following the steps below.

## View your subscription status

At the top of the Subscription tab, a status card shows your current plan, billing period, and how many days remain in your trial or billing cycle. During the 14-day Pro trial, a trial banner also appears at the very top of the Settings page as a reminder.

1. In the left sidebar, click **Settings** (or navigate to `/settings`).
2. Click the **Subscription** tab.
3. Read the status area at the top of the page — it shows your active plan and any relevant dates.

## Compare the three plan tiers

Below the status area, three plan cards display side by side. Each card lists what is included at that tier.

| Plan | Monthly price | Annual price | Highlights |
|------|--------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Starter** | $99/mo | $990/yr | Daily P&L Dashboard, Basic Inventory Tracking, Labor Cost Tracking, POS Integration (Square, Toast, Clover), Recipe Management, Multi-User Access, Email Support |
| **Growth** | $199/mo | $1,990/yr | Everything in Starter, plus Financial Intelligence Dashboard, Inventory Automation (OCR), Recipe & Menu Profitability Analytics, Employee Scheduling, AI Alerts & Anomaly Detection, AI Transaction Categorization, Priority Support |
| **Pro** | $299/mo | $2,990/yr | Everything in Growth, plus AI Assistant, Ops Inbox, Weekly Brief, Bank Account Connections, Automated Transaction Sync, Customer Invoicing, Expense Management, Asset & Equipment Tracking, Payroll Reports & Export, VIP Support |

The **Growth** plan is marked as recommended.

## Toggle between monthly and annual billing

Annual billing saves 17% (approximately two months free) compared to paying month to month. A billing toggle sits between the status area and the plan cards.

1. Go to Settings and click the **Subscription** tab.
2. Find the **Monthly / Annual** toggle in the center of the page.
3. Click the toggle to switch between **Monthly** and **Annual**. The prices on all three plan cards update instantly to reflect your selection.
4. The label next to **Annual** shows **(Save 17%)** as a reminder of the discount.

Choose your preferred billing period before selecting a plan in the next step.

## Select a plan and start checkout

Once you have decided on a tier and billing period, click the plan card to begin the checkout process.

1. Go to Settings and click the **Subscription** tab.
2. Set the billing toggle to **Monthly** or **Annual**.
3. Click the plan card for the tier you want (Starter, Growth, or Pro).
4. You will be taken to the checkout flow to enter your payment information and confirm your subscription.

You can return to this tab at any time to change your plan.

## Understand your volume discount (multi-location operators)

If you manage three or more restaurant locations, a volume discount is automatically applied to your plan price. A green **Volume Discount Applied** notice appears on the page when you qualify, showing the exact percentage off.

| Number of locations | Discount |
|--------------------|---------|
| 3–5 locations | 5% off |
| 6–10 locations | 10% off |
| 11 or more locations | 15% off |

The discount is calculated per location and shown on each plan card after it is applied. No action is needed on your part — the discount appears automatically based on how many restaurants are in your account.

## What happens when you first sign in (Welcome modal)

The first time you sign in to a new EasyShiftHQ account, a **Welcome to EasyShiftHQ** dialog appears. It includes:

- A banner confirming **"You're starting with Pro features FREE for 14 days"**
- A side-by-side comparison of Starter, Growth, and Pro pricing
- The note **"No credit card required to start"**
- A **Get Started** button to close the dialog and enter the app

The Pro plan card in this dialog is highlighted with a **Your Free Trial** badge so you can see which features you have access to during the trial period. You can expand each plan card to see the full feature list by clicking **See all [N] features**.

When you are ready, click **Get Started** to dismiss the modal. The note at the bottom confirms "You can change your plan anytime in Settings."

## Tips

- **Annual billing saves 17%.** If you plan to use EasyShiftHQ long term, switching to annual billing on the Subscription tab is the easiest way to reduce your cost.
- **Volume discounts stack on top of the annual discount.** If you manage multiple locations and choose annual billing, both savings apply.
- **Your trial includes full Pro access.** Take advantage of the 14 days to explore AI Assistant, Bank Account Connections, Payroll Reports, and other Pro features before deciding which tier fits your needs.
- **Only owners see the Subscription tab.** If you cannot find it, check your role with your account owner — see [Roles and What Each One Can Access](/help/roles-and-permissions).

## Troubleshooting

**I cannot see the Subscription tab in Settings.**
The Subscription tab is only visible to users with the **Owner** role. If you are a manager, chef, or staff member, you will not see this tab. Ask the restaurant owner to make plan changes on your behalf.

**The plan cards show a price I did not expect.**
Check the billing toggle near the top of the plan cards. If it is set to **Annual**, prices reflect the discounted annual total. Toggle to **Monthly** to see the standard monthly rate, or leave it on Annual to benefit from the 17% discount.

**I manage multiple locations but do not see a volume discount.**
The green Volume Discount Applied notice only appears when your account has three or more restaurant locations. If you believe you qualify and do not see the discount, confirm that all your locations are added to the same EasyShiftHQ account.

**I clicked a plan card but nothing happened.**
Make sure you are signed in as an owner. If you are the owner and the button still does not respond, try refreshing the page and attempting the checkout again.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need a credit card to start the trial?**
No. Your 14-day Pro trial begins automatically when you create your account. The Welcome modal confirms "No credit card required to start." You only need to enter payment information when you select a paid plan through the Subscription tab.

**What happens when my 14-day trial ends?**
After the trial period, you will need to choose a plan to continue using EasyShiftHQ. Go to Settings > Subscription tab and select the tier that fits your restaurant. Until you choose a plan, access to paid features may be restricted.

**Can I switch plans after I have subscribed?**
Yes. Return to Settings > Subscription tab at any time and click a different plan card to begin the checkout process for a new tier.

**Is there a difference between the annual and monthly plan features?**
No — all three tiers offer the same features regardless of whether you pay monthly or annually. Annual billing simply costs less overall.

**How does the volume discount work if I add a new location later?**
The discount is based on the number of locations in your account at the time of billing. If adding a new location moves you into a higher discount tier (for example, from 2 to 3 locations), the green volume discount notice will appear on the Subscription tab and the reduced price will apply when you next select or renew a plan.
