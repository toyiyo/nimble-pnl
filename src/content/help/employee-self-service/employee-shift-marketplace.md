# Pick Up or Accept a Shift Trade in the Shift Marketplace

The Shift Marketplace lets you browse shifts that your teammates have offered up for trade. When you find one that works for you, accept it in one tap and your manager will be notified to give final approval.

## Before you begin

You must be logged in and linked to an employee profile at your restaurant. If you see a message saying your account is not linked to an employee, ask your manager to connect your login to your employee record before continuing.

## Browse available shifts

1. Go to **Shift Marketplace** at `/employee/shifts`.
2. The **Available Shifts** section lists every open trade posted by your teammates.
3. Each shift tile shows:
   - **Day and date** (for example, Mon, Jun 1)
   - **Position** (for example, Server, Cook)
   - **Start and end time** (for example, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
   - **Total hours** for the shift
   - **Offered by** — the name of the coworker who posted the trade
   - **Reason** — their explanation for why they are giving up the shift (shown when they provided one)
4. If a coworker offered a shift specifically to you, the tile displays an **Offered to you** badge.
5. If there are no open trades, you will see a "No shifts available" message. Check back later — or consider offering one of your own shifts if you need a swap.

## Accept a shift

1. Find a shift tile for an upcoming shift you want to take.
2. Tap the **Accept Shift** button on that tile.
   - The button shows **Accepting...** briefly while your request is being sent.
3. Once the request goes through, you will see a confirmation message: "Trade request accepted — Your manager will review and approve the trade."
4. The shift tile updates immediately: the button is replaced by a **Pending Approval** badge and the note "Awaiting manager review" below it.
5. Your manager will be notified and must approve the trade before it is final. You will receive a notification when the decision is made.

> **Past shifts cannot be accepted.** If a shift has already started, the button reads **Past Shift** and is disabled. You can still see the tile, but you cannot take action on it.

## Return to your personal schedule

Tap **My Schedule** in the top-right corner of the Shift Marketplace page to go back to your own schedule view at `/employee/schedule`.

## Tips

- Shifts you have a scheduling conflict with are still shown in the list, but accepting them is at the manager's discretion during the approval step.
- The marketplace refreshes automatically. You do not need to reload the page to see newly posted shifts.
- Only shifts with status "open" appear in the marketplace — shifts already claimed by someone else and pending approval will no longer show up as available.
- If a coworker directed a trade specifically to you (the **Offered to you** badge), it will appear in your list even if other teammates cannot see it.

## Troubleshooting

**I tapped Accept Shift but got an error.**
Your session may have expired, or there may have been a brief network issue. Refresh the page and try again. If the error message says the trade can no longer be accepted, someone else may have claimed the shift first.

**I do not see any shifts in the marketplace.**
No teammates have posted open trades at this time. The list only shows shifts that are actively available. Check back later or reach out to your manager if you need extra hours.

**My account shows "not linked to an employee."**
Your login is not yet connected to your employee profile. Contact your manager or owner and ask them to link your account before you can use the Shift Marketplace.

**The shift I wanted disappeared.**
Another team member accepted it before you, or the person who posted it cancelled the trade. Keep an eye on the marketplace — new shifts are posted whenever teammates need coverage.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does accepting a shift mean it is confirmed?**
No. Accepting a shift sends a request to your manager for review. The shift is not officially yours until your manager approves it. You will see "Pending Approval / Awaiting manager review" on the tile until then.

**Can I accept a shift that overlaps with one I already have?**
The marketplace does not block you from tapping Accept Shift on a conflicting shift, but your manager will see the overlap during the approval step and may decline the trade.

**What happens after my manager approves the trade?**
The shift is reassigned to you and will appear on your personal schedule. Both you and the coworker who offered the shift will receive a notification about the outcome.

**What if my manager rejects the trade?**
The shift goes back to its original owner and the tile may reappear in the marketplace if they choose to re-post it. You will be notified of the rejection.

**Can I offer my own shifts for trade?**
Posting your own shifts for trade is handled separately — see your schedule page for that option. The Shift Marketplace page is for browsing and accepting shifts that others have already posted.
