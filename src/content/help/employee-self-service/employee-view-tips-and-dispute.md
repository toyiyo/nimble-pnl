# View Your Tips and Dispute an Allocation

The **My Tips** page shows every approved tip allocation you have received, how it was calculated, and whether it has already been paid out to you in cash. If something looks wrong, you can file a report directly from the same page and your manager will be notified automatically.

## Before you begin

- You must be signed in to EasyShiftHQ with a staff or chef account.
- Your account must be linked to an employee record at your restaurant. If you see a message saying your account is not linked, contact your manager — they need to connect your login to your employee profile before **My Tips** is available.
- Tips appear only after a manager has approved the tip split for that day. Pending or unapproved splits will not show up.

---

## Go to My Tips

1. Navigate to **/employee/tips** in the app. The **My Tips** page opens.
2. Your name appears beneath the page title in the header.

---

## Read the period summary

Near the top of the page, two summary figures are displayed side by side for the currently selected period:

- **This period** — the total tip amount you earned across all approved splits in the period.
- **Hours worked** — the total hours credited to you across those same splits.

---

## Navigate to a different period

Below the summary, a period selector shows the start and end dates of the currently displayed range.

- Tap the **left arrow** to move to the previous period.
- Tap the **right arrow** to move to the next period.
- Tap **Today** to jump back to the current period.

---

## Switch between the Breakdown and History tabs

Two tabs sit just below the period selector:

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Breakdown** | A card for each approved tip day, with full detail about your hours, role, amount, and payout status. |
| **History** | A compact chronological list of all approved tips in the selected period. |

Tap a tab label to switch between them.

---

## Read a tip card on the Breakdown tab

Each approved tip day appears as its own card on the **Breakdown** tab. A card shows:

- The **date** of the shift (for example, *Wednesday, May 28*).
- Your **hours worked** for that day (shown with a clock icon) and your **role**.
- The **amount you earned** from the tip pool for that day, displayed on the right.
- A green **Paid [amount] cash** badge if tips for that day have already been distributed to you in cash.

If the restaurant uses a percentage-contribution model, the card also expands to show a four-column breakdown: **Earned**, **Deductions**, **Refunds**, and **Final**.

---

## See how your tips were calculated

On any tip card, tap **How was this calculated?** A dialog titled **How your tips were split** opens and explains the method used for that day:

- **Tips were shared by hours worked.** — You will see your hours, the total team hours, and your exact share percentage.
- **Tips were shared by role.** — You will see your role and a note that role weights determined the distribution.
- **Tips were split manually by your manager.** — Your manager assigned the amount directly.

The dialog also displays your share amount in large text and the total tips that were split that day.

Tap anywhere outside the dialog or press the close button to dismiss it.

---

## Report a problem with a tip allocation

1. On the **Breakdown** tab, find the card for the day you want to dispute.
2. Tap **Something doesn't look right**. The **Report an issue** dialog opens, showing the date at the top.
3. Under **What seems wrong?**, choose one of the five issue types:
   - **Missing hours** — your hours were not counted or were recorded incorrectly.
   - **Incorrect amount** — the dollar amount you received does not look right.
   - **Wrong date** — the tip is assigned to the wrong day.
   - **Missing tips** — you expected a tip entry that is not there.
   - **Other** — anything else.
4. In the **Additional details (optional)** text box, add any extra information that might help your manager investigate.
5. Tap **Submit report**.

A confirmation message appears: *"Issue reported — Your manager has been notified and will review this."* The dialog closes automatically. Your manager receives a notification and will follow up with you directly.

To cancel without submitting, tap **Cancel**.

---

## Check the History tab

The **History** tab shows a **Tip History** list with one row per approved tip in the period. Each row displays:

- The date (for example, *Wed, May 28, 2025*).
- Your hours worked for that day.
- A green **Paid** badge if that day's tips were distributed to you in cash.
- The tip amount on the right.

The History tab is useful for a quick overview without opening individual cards.

---

## Tips

- **Only approved tips appear.** If you worked a shift but see no card for that day, the tip split may still be pending approval by your manager.
- **The "Paid cash" badge confirms in-person distribution.** If the badge is absent, cash may not have been handed out yet or the payout was processed differently — ask your manager for clarification.
- **You can report an issue for any day shown.** The dispute form is available on every approved tip card regardless of how long ago the shift was.
- **Use the period selector to look back.** Tap the left arrow to navigate to previous weeks and review older allocations.

---

## Troubleshooting

**My Tips shows "No tips yet" even though I worked this week.**
The page only shows approved tips. Your manager may not have approved the split for that period yet. Check back later or ask your manager about the status.

**I cannot see the "Something doesn't look right" button.**
The button appears only on cards in the **Breakdown** tab. Switch to the Breakdown tab and locate the card for the day you want to dispute.

**I submitted a report but nothing happened.**
After you tap **Submit report**, a confirmation message appears if the submission was successful. Your manager is notified automatically — they will reach out to you directly. If you saw an error instead, try submitting again. If the problem persists, contact your manager by another means.

**I see an error when I open My Tips.**
Your account may not be linked to an employee profile. Contact your manager and ask them to connect your login to your employee record.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I dispute a tip more than once for the same day?**
Yes — you can tap **Something doesn't look right** on the same card multiple times. Each submission creates a new report, so only submit again if the original issue was not resolved.

**Will my manager know who filed the report?**
Yes. When you submit a dispute, your employee record is included in the report so your manager knows exactly who raised the issue.

**What do the Earned, Deductions, Refunds, and Final columns mean?**
These appear on cards where the restaurant uses a percentage-contribution model. **Earned** is the gross tips credited to you, **Deductions** are amounts removed (for example, tip-out contributions), **Refunds** are amounts added back, and **Final** is what you actually receive.

**What is the difference between the Breakdown and History tabs?**
**Breakdown** shows a detailed card for each day with calculation details and the dispute button. **History** shows a compact list of the same days — useful for scanning totals quickly, but without the ability to see calculation details or file a dispute.

**Why does my "This period" total look different from what I expected?**
The total includes only approved tip splits for the selected period. Unapproved splits, tips from outside the period range, or shifts where you were not included in the pool will not be counted. Use the period selector to make sure you are viewing the correct date range.
