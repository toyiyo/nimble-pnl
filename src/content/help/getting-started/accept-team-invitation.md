# Accept a Team Invitation

This article walks you through accepting an EasyShiftHQ invitation — from clicking the link in your email to landing on the dashboard as a member of your restaurant's team. It applies to all new team members and collaborators.

## Before you begin

- You need an invitation email from EasyShiftHQ. Only a restaurant owner or manager can send one.
- You must use the exact email address the invitation was sent to. The page pre-fills this field and will warn you if there is a mismatch.
- Invitations expire. Accept the invitation before the expiry date passes.

## Step 1: Open the invitation

1. Find the EasyShiftHQ invitation email in your inbox.
2. Click the invitation link in the email.
3. The page loads and shows you a summary with the **restaurant name** and the name of the person who invited you (**Invited by**).

If the page says **Invitation Expired** or **Invalid Invitation**, see the Troubleshooting section below.

## Step 2: Sign in or create an account

What you see next depends on whether you already have an EasyShiftHQ account.

### If you do not have an account yet

You will see a **Create Your Account** form.

**Option A — Continue with Google:**

1. Click the Google button at the top of the form.
2. Choose your Google account. It must match the email address the invitation was sent to.
3. You are joined to the team automatically and redirected to the dashboard.

**Option B — Sign up with email and password:**

1. The **Email** field is already filled in and cannot be changed — this is the address the invitation was sent to.
2. Enter your **Full Name**.
3. Enter a **Password** (at least 6 characters).
4. Click **Join Team**.
5. A confirmation message appears and the page switches to the sign-in form.
6. Enter your **Password** and click **Sign In & Join Team** to complete the process.

### If you already have an account

Click **Already have an account? Sign in instead** near the bottom of the page.

1. The **Email** field is pre-filled and locked to the invitation address.
2. Enter your **Password**.
3. Click **Sign In & Join Team**.

You can also click the Google button on the sign-in view to authenticate with Google instead.

### If you are already signed in

If you open the invitation link while already signed in to EasyShiftHQ, you will see a card showing the invitation details: the **restaurant name** and address (if provided), your assigned **Role**, the name of the person who invited you (**Invited by**), your **Email**, and the **Expires** date.

1. Click **Accept Invitation** to join the team.
2. Click **Decline** if you do not want to join.

If the signed-in email does not match the invitation email, you will see an **Email Mismatch** error. Sign out first, then open the link again and sign in with the correct account.

## Step 3: Confirm you have joined

When everything goes through correctly:

- A **"Welcome to the Team!"** notification appears on screen.
- The page confirms you have successfully joined the restaurant and shows that you are being redirected.
- You are automatically redirected to the dashboard.

## Tips

- **No email verification needed.** Invitation-based sign-ups bypass the usual email confirmation step, so you can get to work right away.
- **Google sign-in is the fastest path.** If your work email is a Google account, using the Google button saves the most steps.
- **Keep the link handy.** If you accidentally close the tab, go back to your invitation email and click the link again — as long as it has not expired, it will still work.
- **Any browser will work.** You do not need to open the link on a specific device.

## Troubleshooting

**"Invitation Expired"**

The link is no longer valid. Ask your manager to resend the invitation from the Team page. Once they send a new one, follow the same steps with the new link.

**"Invalid Invitation"**

The link may have already been used, or the invitation was cancelled. Contact your manager to send a fresh invitation.

**"Email Mismatch" error**

You are signed into EasyShiftHQ with a different email address than the one the invitation was sent to. Sign out of your current account, then open the invitation link again and sign in or create an account using the correct email address.

**The Email field is greyed out and I cannot change it**

This is expected. The invitation is tied to a specific email address, and the field is locked to prevent mistakes. You must use that address to accept.

**I clicked "Join Team" but the page went back to the sign-in form**

Your account was created successfully. Enter your new password and click **Sign In & Join Team** to finish joining.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I use a different email address than the one in the invitation?**

No. EasyShiftHQ ties invitations to a specific email address. If you need the invitation sent to a different address, ask your manager to cancel the current invitation and send a new one to the correct address.

**Do I need to verify my email before I can log in?**

No. Invitation-based sign-ups do not require an email verification step. You can sign in immediately after creating your account.

**What role will I have after I join?**

You will be assigned the role that was set when the invitation was created. Your manager sets this when they send the invitation. To learn what each role can access, see [Roles and What Each One Can Access](/help/roles-and-permissions).

**Can I accept the same invitation more than once?**

No. Once an invitation has been accepted, the link becomes invalid. If you need to re-join a restaurant, ask your manager to send a new invitation.

**What if I never received the invitation email?**

Check your spam or junk folder first. If it is not there, ask your manager to resend the invitation from the Team page. Each resend generates a fresh link.
