::finding:: severity=critical file=supabase/migrations/20260720120000_shift_fill_by_assignment.sql line=522
`approve_open_shift_claim` is `SECURITY DEFINER` and fetches the claim by `id` only, with no `auth.uid()`/manager membership check before creating the shift and marking the claim approved. Because the earlier `GRANT EXECUTE ... TO authenticated` is preserved by `CREATE OR REPLACE FUNCTION`, any authenticated user who obtains or guesses a pending `open_shift_claims.id` can call this RPC and approve a claim in another restaurant, bypassing the RLS manager policy entirely.
