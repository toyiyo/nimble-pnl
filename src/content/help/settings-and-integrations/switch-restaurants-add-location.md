# Switch Between Restaurants or Add a New Location

If your account covers more than one location, EasyShiftHQ lets you move between them instantly using the restaurant picker in the top navigation bar. Owners can also create a brand-new location without leaving the app.

## Before you begin

- **Switching locations** is available to all roles (owner, manager, chef, and staff) for any restaurant you have been given access to.
- **Creating a new location** is available only to **owners**. Managers, chefs, and staff will not see the "Create new restaurant" option. If you need a new location added, ask your owner to do it.
- First-time users who have not yet set up any restaurant can also create their first location.

## Switch to a different restaurant

1. Look at the top navigation bar. You will see the name of your currently active restaurant next to a small building icon.
2. Click on that restaurant name to open the dropdown. The menu shows **Your Restaurants** along with a count of how many locations you have access to.
3. Click the name of the location you want to work in.
4. The app immediately loads data for that location. EasyShiftHQ remembers your choice, so the same restaurant is automatically selected the next time you log in.

## Add a new restaurant location (owners only)

1. Click the restaurant name in the top navigation bar to open the dropdown.
2. At the bottom of the list, click **Create new restaurant**. This option appears only if you have the owner role on at least one existing restaurant (or you have no restaurants yet).
3. A dialog titled **Create New Restaurant** opens. Fill in the fields:
   - **Name** (required) — the name of the new location.
   - **Address** — the street address (optional).
   - **Phone** — the location's phone number (optional).
   - **Cuisine** — a description of the cuisine type (optional).
   - **Timezone** (required) — the timezone the location operates in. This defaults to your browser's current timezone; change it if the new location is in a different zone.
4. If you already have at least one restaurant on your account and are on a paid plan, a **billing preview** appears below the form. It shows your current subscription cost and what it will become after adding this location, so you know the exact impact before you confirm.
   - If adding this location unlocks a volume discount, the preview will highlight the savings.
   - If you are close to a volume-discount milestone, the preview will tell you how many more locations you need to reach it.
5. Click **Create Restaurant** to save. The button label changes to **Creating...** while the location is being set up. Once complete, the dialog closes and the new restaurant is available in your picker.
6. To close without saving at any point, click **Cancel**.

## Tips

- Your restaurant selection is remembered per device and per account — if you sign in on a different device or browser, you will need to select the location once before the app remembers it on that device.
- If you only have one restaurant on your account, EasyShiftHQ selects it for you automatically when you log in; you do not need to choose from the picker.
- You can update a location's name, address, phone, and other details after creation from **Settings**.

## Troubleshooting

**I do not see "Create new restaurant" in the dropdown.**
This option is visible only to owners. If you are a manager, chef, or staff member, ask your account owner to add the new location.

**The dropdown shows a restaurant I cannot access.**
You will only see restaurants you have been granted access to. If a location is missing, ask the owner of that location to invite you.

**The app is showing data from the wrong location.**
Click the restaurant name in the top bar and select the correct location from the list. EasyShiftHQ will immediately switch all data to the chosen restaurant.

**I got an error when clicking "Create Restaurant".**
Make sure the **Name** and **Timezone** fields are filled in — both are required. If the error persists, check your internet connection and try again.

## Frequently asked questions

**Will switching restaurants log me out or lose my work?**
No. Switching only changes which location's data you are viewing. Any unsaved work in a form on the current page may be lost, so save before switching if needed.

**Does adding a new location change my subscription cost immediately?**
The billing preview shown in the dialog tells you exactly what your new cost will be. The change takes effect when you click **Create Restaurant**.

**Can I delete a restaurant location I no longer need?**
Deletion is managed from **Settings**. Reach out to support if you do not see this option.

**Why does the app automatically select a restaurant when I log in?**
If you have only one location, EasyShiftHQ selects it for you automatically. If you have multiple locations, the app remembers whichever one you were last using.

**What is the trial countdown badge I see in the top bar?**
If your account is on a free trial, a badge showing how many days remain (for example, **5 days left**) appears in the top navigation bar. Click it to go directly to the **Subscription** tab in Settings where you can choose a plan before the trial ends. The badge turns amber when 7 days or fewer remain and red when 3 days or fewer remain.
