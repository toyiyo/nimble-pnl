## 9. Responsive Design

### Desktop (>1024px)
- Full schedule grid visible
- All days shown horizontally
- Template cards in 3-column grid

### Tablet (768-1024px)
- Horizontal scroll for schedule
- Sticky employee column
- Template cards in 2-column grid

### Mobile (<768px)
- Vertical scroll for schedule
- Collapsible employee sections
- Template cards in 1-column stack
- Touch-friendly drag handles

### Shift Status
- **Scheduled**: Gray badge
- **Confirmed**: Blue badge
- **Completed**: Green badge
- **Cancelled**: Red badge

### Selection
- **Selected**: Blue ring (2px)
- **Hover**: Light blue background
- **Drag**: Semi-transparent (50% opacity)
- **Drop Zone**: Blue glow

### Templates
- **Active**: Green indicator
- **Inactive**: Gray indicator (future feature)

## Animation Timings

- **Drag pickup**: 150ms ease-out
- **Drop**: 200ms ease-in-out
- **Hover effects**: 100ms ease
- **Tab transition**: 200ms ease
- **Toast**: Slide in 300ms, fade out 200ms
