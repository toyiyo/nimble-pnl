# Reset or Change Your Password

This article explains how to regain access to your account when you've forgotten your password, and how to update your password from within the app while you're already signed in. Both paths are available to all roles.

## Before you begin

- To use the forgot-password flow, you need access to the email address associated with your EasyShiftHQ account.
- If you signed up using Google ("Sign in with Google"), password management is not available — see the note in the [Google sign-in users](#google-sign-in-users) section below.

## Reset a forgotten password

Use this flow when you cannot sign in because you've forgotten your password.

1. Go to the EasyShiftHQ sign-in page.
2. Click **Forgot password?** (below the sign-in form).
3. On the reset page, enter your account email address in the **Email Address** field.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.
5. A confirmation screen appears with the heading **Check Your Email**. EasyShiftHQ has sent a password reset link to the address you entered.
6. Open your email inbox and find the message from EasyShiftHQ. If you don't see it, check your spam or junk folder.
7. Click the link in the email. The link is valid for **1 hour** — if it has expired, repeat the steps above to request a new one.
8. The link opens the EasyShiftHQ reset page. You will see the subtitle **Create a new password for your account** below the EasyShiftHQ logo.
9. Enter your new password in the **New Password** field. Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.
10. Re-enter the same password in the **Confirm New Password** field.
11. Click **Reset Password**.
12. A **Password Reset Successful** confirmation appears, and the app automatically redirects you to the dashboard within a few seconds.

## Change your password while logged in

Use this flow when you know your current password and simply want to update it.

1. Sign in to EasyShiftHQ.
2. Navigate to **Settings** (available from the main menu).
3. Open the **Security** tab. You will see the **Security Settings** card.
4. Enter your new password in the **New Password** field. Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.
5. Re-enter the same password in the **Confirm New Password** field.
6. Click **Update Password**.
7. A confirmation notification appears confirming your password has been successfully changed. The fields clear automatically.

## Google sign-in users

If your account is linked to Google ("Sign in with Google"), the **Security Settings** card in Settings displays the following notice:

> "You're signed in with Google OAuth. Password management is not available for OAuth accounts."

To manage your password, visit your Google account settings directly. EasyShiftHQ does not store or manage passwords for Google-linked accounts.

## Tips

- Choose a password you haven't used on other websites to keep your account secure.
- Store your new password in a password manager so you don't need to use the forgot-password flow again.
- The reset link from the email is single-use — once you've set a new password, the link can't be used a second time.
- If you share a device with other staff, sign out after your session to protect your account.

## Troubleshooting

**I didn't receive the reset email.**
Wait a minute and refresh your inbox. Check your spam, junk, or promotions folders. Make sure you entered the exact email address associated with your account. If the problem persists, try the reset flow again.

**The reset link says it is invalid or has expired.**
Links expire after 1 hour. Return to the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?**, and request a new link.

**I see "Passwords Don't Match" when I click Reset Password or Update Password.**
The text in **New Password** and **Confirm New Password** must be identical, including capitalization. Re-enter both fields carefully and try again.

**I see "Invalid Password" when I submit.**
Your password does not meet the requirements. Make sure it is at least 8 characters long and contains at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.

**The Security Settings tab shows a Google OAuth notice instead of the password form.**
Your account is connected through Google. Password management must be done through Google — EasyShiftHQ cannot change it for you.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can any role reset their own password?**
Yes. All roles — owner, manager, chef, and staff — can use both the forgot-password flow and the in-app Security Settings to manage their own password.

**How long does the reset link last?**
The link in the email is valid for 1 hour from the time it was sent. After that, you need to request a new one.

**Will I be signed out of other devices after resetting my password?**
After your password is reset, the app redirects you to the dashboard. For security, it is good practice to sign out of any other shared devices using your account.

**Can I reuse my old password?**
The app does not prevent you from entering your previous password, but using a unique password you haven't used before is recommended for security.

**What if I signed up with Google and forgot which email I used?**
Your EasyShiftHQ account is tied to your Google account. Sign in by clicking "Sign in with Google" and selecting the correct Google account from the list.
