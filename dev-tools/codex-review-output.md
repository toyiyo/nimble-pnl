::finding:: severity=major file=src/hooks/useLaborPnlAnalytics.ts line=34
`/labor` always fetches and summarizes a 12-week window, regardless of the Day/Week/Month toggle. Because `series` is built from that full `useLaborPnlCore(restaurantId, WEEKS)` result at line 37 and `summary` is then computed from the whole series, opening `/labor` on the default `Day` view shows 12 weeks of daily buckets and 12-week KPI totals, not the current day/hour-of-day view described by the design. Trigger: any restaurant with more than one day of sales/labor history opens `/labor`; the “Day” KPI row and chart are inflated by historical weeks.
