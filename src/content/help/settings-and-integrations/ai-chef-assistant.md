# Use the AI Chef Assistant to Ask Questions About Your Restaurant

The Chef Assistant is a conversational AI built into EasyShiftHQ that answers questions about your restaurant using your actual live data — sales, inventory, recipes, labor, financials, and more — right inside the app.

## Before you begin

- **Plan requirement:** The Chef Assistant is a Pro plan feature. If you are on a free plan, the panel shows an **Upgrade to Pro** button instead of a chat interface. Clicking it takes you to **Settings > Subscription**.
- **Required role:** The Chef Assistant is visible only to owners, managers, and chefs. Staff and kiosk users do not see the chat icon.
- **Restaurant selection:** A restaurant must be selected before you can chat. If no restaurant is selected, the panel prompts you to choose one.

## Open and position the Chef Assistant

1. Look for the floating chef hat icon in the bottom-right corner of any page.
2. Click the icon to open the **Chef Assistant** panel on the right side of the screen.
3. If the icon shows a small green dot, the panel is minimized. Click the icon again to expand it back to full view.
4. To move the icon out of the way, click and drag it to any position on the screen. The new position is saved automatically.

## Ask a question

1. Click inside the **Message...** field at the bottom of the chat panel.
2. Type your question in plain language.
3. Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button (the arrow icon) to submit.
4. The assistant streams its response in real time. While it is typing, you will see a "Cooking up a response..." indicator.
5. To stop a response mid-stream, click the **stop** button (the circle with an X) that appears in place of the Send button while the assistant is working.

## Use quick-start badges

When you open a new conversation, four quick-start badges appear in the center of the chat area:

- **Show KPIs** — loads key metrics for the current month
- **Inventory Status** — checks your current stock levels
- **Recipe Analytics** — ranks recipes by profitability
- **Sales Summary** — summarizes sales for the current week

Click any badge to pre-fill that prompt in the message field, then send it.

## What you can ask about

The Chef Assistant pulls from your actual restaurant data. Here are the topics it understands:

### Sales and revenue
- "Summarize sales for this week"
- "Show me daily sales totals for last month"
- "How did this week compare to last week?"

### Inventory
- "What is my current inventory status?"
- "Which items are running low?"

### Recipes
- "Which recipes are most profitable?"
- "What is my average food cost percentage?"

### Financials
- "Show me an income statement for this month" (owners and managers only)
- "What is my cash flow?" (owners and managers only)
- "What are my pending outflows?" (owners and managers only)

### Labor and payroll
- "What are my labor costs this week?"
- "Show me time punches for today" (owners and managers only)
- "Give me a payroll summary" (owners and managers only)

### Scheduling
- "Give me a schedule overview for this week"

### Tips
- "Show me the tip summary for last week" (owners and managers only)

### Break-even
- "How am I tracking against break-even this month?" (owners and managers only)

## Manage conversations

Your last 20 conversations are saved automatically.

### Open the conversation sidebar
1. Click the panel icon in the top-left of the chat header (to the left of the Chef Assistant title) to open the conversation sidebar.
2. Click it again to hide the sidebar.

### Start a new conversation
1. Open the conversation sidebar.
2. Click **New chat**.

### Switch to a previous conversation
1. Open the conversation sidebar.
2. Click any conversation in the list to load its messages.

### Delete a conversation
1. Open the conversation sidebar.
2. Hover over the conversation you want to remove.
3. Click the **trash** icon that appears on the right side of that row.
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

## Tips

- The assistant uses your real data — results reflect your actual sales, inventory, and labor records, not estimates.
- You can ask follow-up questions in the same conversation and the assistant remembers what was said earlier in that thread.
- For time punch details, payroll summaries, tip summaries, and break-even progress, you need the owner or manager role. Chefs can ask about sales, inventory, recipes, aggregate labor costs, and schedule overviews.
- You can resize the chat panel by dragging the left edge left or right to give yourself more reading room.

## Troubleshooting

**The chat icon is not visible.**
The Chef Assistant is hidden for staff and kiosk roles. If you are an owner, manager, or chef and still do not see the icon, check that you have a restaurant selected. Also confirm that your account is on the Pro plan — go to **Settings > Subscription** to verify.

**The panel shows "Upgrade to Pro" instead of a chat field.**
Your account is on the free plan. Click **Upgrade to Pro** to go to the subscription settings page.

**The assistant says it cannot find data.**
Make sure the date range you are asking about falls within a period when your restaurant was active and data was being recorded. If you recently connected a POS or bank account, allow a few minutes for initial data to sync.

**The response stopped partway through.**
Click the stop button if you want to cancel early, or simply send a follow-up message asking the assistant to continue or clarify.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is the Chef Assistant reading live data or cached data?**
It reads your actual data at the time you ask the question. Results for the current day or week will reflect whatever has been recorded up to that moment.

**Can staff employees use the Chef Assistant?**
No. The icon is hidden for staff and kiosk roles. Only owners, managers, and chefs can access it.

**How many conversations are saved?**
The sidebar shows your 20 most recent conversations. Older conversations are no longer listed but can be started fresh with **New chat**.

**Can I ask questions about a specific date range?**
Yes. You can specify dates naturally, for example: "Show me sales from May 1 to May 15" or "What were my labor costs last month?"

**Does the Chef Assistant make changes to my data?**
No. The Chef Assistant only reads your data to answer questions. It does not create, edit, or delete any records.
