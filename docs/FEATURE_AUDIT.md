# EasyShiftHQ Feature Audit

> **Purpose**: Comprehensive list of all functionality provided to restaurant managers.
> **Last Updated**: January 2026
> **Use**: Landing page content, marketing materials, feature documentation

## Financial Management

### Real-Time Dashboard
- **Live P&L Overview** - See net revenue, COGS, labor costs, and gross profit updated in real-time
- **Critical Alerts System** - Automatic warnings for cash runway, prime cost thresholds, inventory issues, and operational anomalies
- **Monthly Performance Trends** - Historical comparison charts showing month-over-month performance
- **Revenue Breakdown** - Category-level revenue analysis with unmapped item tracking
- **Cashflow Visualization** - Interactive Sankey chart showing money flow through your business
- **Banking Snapshot** - At-a-glance view of all connected bank account balances
- **Outflow Analysis** - Spending breakdown by category and top vendors
- **Break-Even Tracking** - Daily break-even analysis with historical comparison
- **AI-Powered Insights** - Intelligent recommendations based on your restaurant's data

### Bank Account Management
- **Secure Bank Connections** - Connect bank accounts via Stripe Financial Connections with bank-level security
- **Real-Time Balance Sync** - Automatic balance updates across all connected accounts
- **Multi-Account Support** - Manage checking, savings, and credit accounts in one place
- **Total Balance Aggregation** - See your complete financial picture across all accounts

### Transaction Management
- **Automatic Transaction Import** - Daily sync of all bank transactions
- **AI-Powered Categorization** - Machine learning suggests categories based on your patterns
- **Bulk Categorization** - Categorize multiple transactions at once with one click
- **Smart Category Rules** - Create rules to auto-categorize recurring transactions
- **Transaction Search & Filtering** - Find any transaction by payee, amount, date, or category
- **Reconciliation Tools** - Mark transactions as transfers, exclude non-business items
- **Export Capabilities** - Download transactions as CSV or PDF for accountants

### Chart of Accounts
- **Restaurant-Specific Categories** - Pre-configured chart of accounts for food service businesses
- **Account Hierarchy** - Organized structure for revenue, COGS, expenses, and assets
- **Balance Tracking** - Real-time balances for each account category
- **Custom Account Creation** - Add accounts specific to your operation

### Financial Statements
- **Income Statement** - Automated P&L generation for any date range
- **Balance Sheet** - Asset, liability, and equity reporting
- **Cash Flow Statement** - Track cash movements in and out of your business
- **Trial Balance** - Verification reports for accounting accuracy
- **Period Comparison** - Compare statements across different time periods
