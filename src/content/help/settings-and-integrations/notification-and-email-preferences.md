# Manage Email Notifications and the Weekly Brief

EasyShiftHQ can send automated emails when staff submit, approve, or reject time-off requests, and deliver a Monday morning summary of your restaurant's performance. This article explains how to turn each option on or off and how to stop receiving emails entirely.

## Before you begin

You must be signed in as an **owner** or **manager** to access Notification Settings. Staff and other roles cannot see or change these preferences.

## Open Notification Settings

1. In the left navigation, click **Settings**.
2. At the top of the page, click the **Notifications** tab.

The Notifications tab is visible only to owners and managers. If you do not see it, contact your restaurant owner to adjust your role.

## Control time-off request notifications

Under the **Time-Off Request Notifications** section, three individual toggles let you choose which events trigger an email.

1. **New Request Submitted** — turn this on to send an email each time a team member creates a time-off request.
2. **Request Approved** — turn this on to send an email when a request is approved.
3. **Request Rejected** — turn this on to send an email when a request is rejected.

Toggle any switch to the right to enable it, or to the left to disable it. Changes are not saved until you click **Save Settings** (see below).

## Set who receives time-off emails

Under the **Notification Recipients** section, two toggles control the audience for every time-off email.

1. **Notify Managers** — when on, EasyShiftHQ sends the email to all owners and managers on this restaurant's team.
2. **Notify Employee** — when on, EasyShiftHQ sends the email to the employee who submitted the request.

You can turn on either or both toggles independently.

### Warning: no approvers configured

If **Notify Managers** is on but your restaurant has no owners or managers set up yet, a yellow warning appears:

> **No approvers configured** — This restaurant has no owners or managers set up to receive notifications. Invite a teammate with owner or manager access from the Team page.

Follow the link to the Team page to invite a teammate before saving.

## Turn the Weekly Brief on or off

Under the **Weekly Brief** section, one toggle controls your Monday digest.

1. Find the **Weekly Brief Email** toggle.
2. Toggle it on to receive a Monday morning email with key metrics, variances, and action items for your restaurant.
3. Toggle it off to stop receiving those emails.

The Weekly Brief toggle saves immediately when you flip it — you do not need to click **Save Settings** afterward.

## Save or discard your changes

After adjusting any toggle in the **Time-Off Request Notifications** or **Notification Recipients** sections:

1. Click **Save Settings** to apply your changes. The button shows a checkmark icon and is labeled "Save Settings."
2. Click **Reset Changes** to revert all unsaved toggles back to their last saved values. This button appears only when you have unsaved changes.

The Weekly Brief toggle saves on its own and is not affected by **Save Settings** or **Reset Changes**.

## Opt out of all EasyShiftHQ emails

Each EasyShiftHQ email includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom.

1. Open any EasyShiftHQ email and click the unsubscribe link at the bottom.
2. The page at `/unsubscribe` shows the heading **"You're unsubscribed"** and confirms that EasyShiftHQ will no longer send you emails from that list.
3. If you change your mind, reply to any EasyShiftHQ email and the team will add you back.

If the unsubscribe link appears broken or missing required information, the page will say **"Invalid unsubscribe link"** and ask you to reply to the email for manual removal.

## Tips

- Email notifications go only to addresses registered in each person's EasyShiftHQ profile. Ask team members to verify their email address if they are not receiving expected notifications.
- You can have **Notify Managers** and **Notify Employee** both on at the same time — the right people will each get their own copy.
- The Weekly Brief toggle is per-restaurant, so if you manage multiple locations you can enable it for some and not others.

## Troubleshooting

**I toggled a switch but no email was sent.**
Check that you clicked **Save Settings** after making changes to the time-off toggles. Also confirm that the recipient has a valid email address in their profile.

**The Notifications tab is not visible.**
The tab appears only for owners and managers. Ask your restaurant owner to upgrade your role.

**I see the "No approvers configured" warning.**
Go to the Team page and invite at least one person with an owner or manager role, then return to Notifications and save.

**The unsubscribe link did not work.**
Reply directly to any EasyShiftHQ email and request manual removal. The support team will unsubscribe you promptly.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I silence just one type of notification, like approvals?**
Yes. Each event — New Request Submitted, Request Approved, and Request Rejected — has its own toggle, so you can enable or disable them individually.

**Does disabling "Notify Managers" also stop the Weekly Brief?**
No. The Weekly Brief is a separate toggle under the Weekly Brief section and is not affected by the time-off recipient settings.

**If I unsubscribe via email, will the Settings page still show the toggle as on?**
The in-app toggle reflects the restaurant-level preference, not your personal email opt-out. After using the unsubscribe link you will not receive emails even if the toggle appears on.

**Do staff members see the Notifications tab?**
No. The Notifications tab is only shown to owners and managers.

**Can I re-enable the Weekly Brief after turning it off?**
Yes. Go back to Settings, click the Notifications tab, and flip the **Weekly Brief Email** toggle on again.
